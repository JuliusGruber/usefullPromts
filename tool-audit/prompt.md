# Tool Audit

Ask your coding agent to audit its own environment:

> **"What tools are you missing to be effective on my machine? Analyze what's installed, what's missing, what's broken, what's redundant. Prioritize by impact on your ability to help me."**

## When to Use

- When setting up a new machine or dev environment for AI-assisted coding.
- After installing or updating your AI coding assistant.
- Periodically, to keep your agent's tooling optimized as your workflow evolves.
